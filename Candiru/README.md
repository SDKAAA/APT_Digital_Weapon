**NAME:**  
Candiru
  
**Alias**  
Caramel Tsunami, SOURGUM
  
**Description**:   
The earliest attack activities of this group can be traced back to 2021. Suspected to be a mercenary group from Israel, they primarily target politicians, human rights activists, journalists, scholars, embassies, news targets, etc., in Israel, Iran, Lebanon, Yemen, Spain (Catalonia), the UK, Palestine, Turkey, Armenia, and Singapore. In their attacks, they have repeatedly used 0day vulnerabilities related to Windows/Chrome and delivered malicious code from the DevilsTongue family.
