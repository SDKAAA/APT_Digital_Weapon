**NAME:**  
APT-C-64
  
**Description**:   
The group's earliest attack activities can be traced back to 2006, suspected to be from Taiwan, China, primarily targeting digital media service systems of government and enterprises in mainland China and the Hong Kong and Macao regions, as well as related websites, outdoor electronic screens, internet TV, and more.
