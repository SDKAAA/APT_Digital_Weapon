**NAME:**  
GreedyBear
  
  
**Description**:   
The group's earliest attack activities can be traced back to 2025. Suspected to be from Russia, the main targets of the attacks are digital wallets and industries related to cryptocurrency users. In this attack, over 150 malicious Firefox extensions, nearly 500 malicious executable files, and phishing websites masquerading as cryptocurrency services were used to steal more than $1 million worth of cryptocurrency assets.
