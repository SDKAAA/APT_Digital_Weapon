**NAME**:
Cyber Partisans
  
**Description**:
The group's earliest attack activities can be traced back to 2020, suspected to originate from Belarus, primarily targeting Russian and Belarusian industrial enterprises and government agencies, and is known for launching multiple cyber attacks against the authoritarian Belarusian government.
