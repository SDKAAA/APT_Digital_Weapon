**NAME**:
LapDogs
  
**Description**:
The group's earliest attack activities can be traced back to September 2023, with an unknown origin, primarily targeting devices in the real estate, information technology, internet services, and media sectors in the United States, Southeast Asia, especially Japan, South Korea, Hong Kong, and Taiwan, and maintaining these compromised devices as covert long-term infrastructure, i.e., Operational Relay Node (ORN) networks, which have been used at least by the APT named UAT-5918.
